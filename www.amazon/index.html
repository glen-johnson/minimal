Frequently Asked Questions

about Searching

1. I want more results!

Describing a product is not the same as asking Google or Siri a question!

Adjectives may have many synonyms and your search will only find websites that use the same synonyms you are using.

We require product descriptions to be 3 words or less to capture the best stores competing for your business.

• Instead of 'flea treatments, try just 'fleas'.

• Instead of 'larimar necklace', try just 'larimar'.

• Instead of 'honda accord seat covers', try 'honda seat covers'.

• Try product synonyms: search both 'apple cases' and 'iphone folio'.

• Compound words with spaces can bring up more results: search both 'shopvac' and 'shop vac.'

BTW, these suggestions will give you better shopping results on any search engine!

2. The Highlight button appears to not do anything. Why?

It takes a few seconds for your device to find and highlight your search words. If there are no search words in the results, then it will appear to have done nothing.

Kindle Fires take an obsurd length of time to highlight, most other devices work quickly.

3. Where do the 'More Pages' links come from?

These are additional links to some of the underlying pages of the website.

4. Are my searches being recorded?

No. There is no storage, analysis, or correlation of your search words, search history, store visits or IP address.

5. How do I do local searches?

Amazon-alternatives does not track or request your whereabouts using your device. Append the city to your search, e.g. 'real estate agents Peoria'.

6. Does clicking on a website link incur a cost to the website owner?

No, Amazon-alternatives does not use Pay-per-Click.

7. I see ads related to my search appearing in other websites I later visit after using Amazon-alternatives. Why?

You have visited a site in the Amazon-alternatives search results that sold your IP address and what you are shopping for to a third party. Amazon-alternatives itself does not participate in such programs.

about Small Businesses

1. Where does Amazon-alternatives find small businesses?

Some businesses are entered by users like you and others are discovered by us.
To enter your business or any business into Amazon-alternatives, type the domain name like 'myFavoriteStore.com' into the search bar.

2. Are these small businesses safe to buy from?

Phone the business if this is your first purchase and the cost is high. Use PayPal or a credit card.

3. Why buy from a small business rather than Amazon?

Small businesses have customer support, product experts, a telephone and they answer it. Not so at Amazon.

4. Amazon always ships fast. How do I know I will get the same service?

If you want something in a hurry, call the business and ask if your item is in stock and to please ship it immediately.

5. I had a bad experience with a partiular business. What can I do?

about Amazon-alternatives

1. What is Amazon-alternatives?

Amazon-alternatives finds up to 200 other businesess for products entered into the search bar.

2. Doesn't Google and Bing do this?

Of course, but Amazon-alternatives does not factor in popularity metrics, allowing less known businesses to be on the first page.

3. If Amazon-alternatives is all about small business, why are Amazon and Google buttons on all search result pages?

This feature makes the website much more useful. The more useful a website is, the more traffic it will get.

about websites and search hits

1. Why doesn't the Amazon-alternatives index all my website's pages automatically?

To prevent sites like Walmart.com and Amazon.com from dominating search results, Amazon-alternatives limits each website to 60 indexed pages.

2. I have more than one page on my website that matches a product search but only see one page from my website in the search results. Why?

To expose more businesses to more shoppers, each website is allowed one result page on any search. The page chosen has the best relevance to the search terms.

3. How do I find what pages Amazon-alternatives has indexed for a particular website?

On the search page, enter just the domain name, e.g. 'beachncreations.com'.

4. How can I prevent Amazon-alternatives from indexing my website?

Disallow '/' for User-Agent 'amazonalternatives' in your website's robots.txt file.

5. How does Amazon-alternatives rank search results?

Apache Solr is our search engine core. It is used unmodified. After Solr returns a set of search results, we boost the score of promoted websites because we know these websites are active and current.

about crawling and database updates

1. I changed my website. When will Amazon-alternatives see my updates?

Our database is refreshed about once a week. Indexed pages will be updated then.

2. My website was rejected by Amazon-alternatives after I entered my domain name. What can I do?

If the reason is fixable as indicated in the index results, please fix your website and enter the domain name again into the search bar. It will be re-examined and indexed if corrected.
