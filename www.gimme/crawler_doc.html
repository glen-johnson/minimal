About Our Web Crawler

What is GimmeUSA?

GimmeUSA is a search engine for alternatives to Amazon.com.

What is User Agent of the robot?

Mozilla/5.0 (compatible; GimmeUSAbot/1.0; +http://gimmeusa.com/crawler.html)

Responsible Party

GimmieUSA Hermosa Beach, California is the owner and operator of this web crawler or robot.

Purpose

If your website is a U.S. a on-line seller, shoppers will be able to find it on GimmeUSA.com

Business website identification

If the information on your Homepage or About page reveals that it is not an on-line seller, no information is saved and crawling your site stops. Otherwise, your business may be found on GimmeUSA.com unless you specify '/' for User-Agent 'gimmeusa' in your robots.txt disallow directives.

Crawl frequency for business websites

Your homepage and a select few underlying pages are crawled no more than once per week to sync GimmeUSA with your website updates. When the underlying pages are fetched, the frequency may be high but should not be less than 2 seconds between each page request.
